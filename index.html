🏆 Smart India Hackathon 2023

Agro Farms

India has over 100 million small-scale farmers who struggle daily with labour shortages and lack of affordable equipment. Agro Farms is an Android application built during the Smart India Hackathon to bridge this gap — creating a digital marketplace where farmers can instantly find and hire workers, source second-hand equipment, and get real-time crop and weather insights, all from their phone.

  • Hire Workers module linking farmers with workers & tractor drivers
  • Second Hand Store for affordable agricultural equipment access
  • Weather Prediction and Crop Insights modules for data-driven farming
  • Intuitive login, worker search & direct phone-based communication flows
